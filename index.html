Did you know that there's just a few months left to enter the Global Franchise Awards 2022? If you're yet to submit your franchise brand to be in with the chance of winning big, then don't worry: you can head to our dedicated award website to obtain your entry form!

To help you ensure this form is as comprehensive and impressive as can be, we've gathered some frequently asked questions among previous entrants below:

What is important to include on an entry form?

As well as making sure you respond to each of the questions on the entry form, true frontrunners include real-world, tangible examples alongside their responses. These support your claims and allow our judging panel to get a real picture of your brand, and its impact in the wider franchising community.

Testimonials also work very well on an entry form, because they showcase examples of how your franchise brand, marketing firm, law firm, or consultancy has had a true impact on the individuals it engages.

It’s also highly recommended that you outline your brand’s international presence and expansion strategy, including the countries that your franchise operates within and any upcoming plans you have in the pipeline. Our judges will be looking for concepts that succeed on a truly global scale.

What other materials should I provide alongside my entry form?

Some of our strongest entrants in recent years have included marketing materials and business growth plans alongside their entry form, to provide a visual representation of their brand.

Not only does this show evidence to support the claims made in your entry form, but it allows our judging panel to ascertain the visual language used by your brand – and compare it to other entrants within your category.

Remember: the more information you can provide, the better. Just remember to note the suggested word count of each text section on your entry form, and stick to this as closely as possible.

How do I enter the Global Franchise Champion or Contribution to International Franchising Awards?

The easy answer: you already have!

These two special categories are judged by our internal awards team alongside our panel of judges, and by entering your brand into one of our other 12 categories, you’ll automatically be placed into consideration for these awards.

How do this year’s Regional Champion awards work?

These new categories work similarly to the above. By entering your brand into any of our other categories and specifying where your headquarters is based, you’ll automatically be placed into consideration for our Regional Champion accolades.

A representative from each region will judge finalists alongside our internal awards team, and upon deliberation, a winner from each of our four Regional Champion markets – Americas, Europe, Middle East & Africa, or Asia-Pacific – will be crowned. For this reason, make sure that your entry form is as flawless as it can be, so that you stand-out for these additional considerations!

Don’t forget that you need to send your entry form over by January 20, 2022. Entry forms sent after this time will unfortunately not be considered by our judging panel.
